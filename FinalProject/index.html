What is Lava Floor Films? (11/5/2013)


Lava Floor Films is an idea Jerad Potthoff and myself thought up in March of 2013 that would help enhance the local Erie, PA music scene. After a short while we decided that it would probably be best if we didn't limit ourselves to only one area. This is when we realized what we truly wanted Lava Floor Films to be. We are going to be a place where local bands and fans can watch videos of bands from around their area. The full scope of this project will not be achieved for a while, but we are hard at work making this your main source for all local music. With competitions, news, forums, interviews, music videos, live videos, and merchandise we are very excited at the impact this site will be able to make on local music.
